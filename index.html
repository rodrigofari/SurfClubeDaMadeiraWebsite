Plan of our Surf Lesson

Arrival

If you booked a place we pick up in Santa Luzia Park in Funchal. If you didn't book transportation you can meet as at Alagoa Beach in Porto da Cruz.

Before Surf Lesson

First we start from giving you the proper surf equipment :) Then after changing into wetsuits we start the warm-up, we teach you the technique of "pop up" and explain the safety and rules of the beach that we are going to surf in.

Surf Lesson

After the warm-up, technique and safety briefing we take you straight to the ocean where for 1,5 hour our instructors will help you to catch as many waves as possible!
